Problem

Throughout the creation of this website, I came across one main obstacle that caused the completion of this website to be withheld longer than expected. While completing this site, I could not figure out how to keep content in the same placement when maximizing and minimizing the screen. If this happens, whenever you were to load the website onto different devices, the content will look chaotic and out of place.

Process

The process of building this site, for the most part, went smoothly. First, I organized all of my content on a Google Doc to make the process go a little quicker once I began coding. After organizing all of my content, I started to code. I feel as though the challenges I had while creating this website made the result that much more satisfying in the end.
